Animal Squad

 Turn based strategy game in which you can fight a opponent to finally prove that you are the better tactician! Choose 5 animals each with completly unqiue skills and try to combine their attacks to powerfull combos to gain the upperhand in battle. Win your fights by managing positioning, objectives and healthpoints. This project was made as end project at SAE Vienna in about 6 month. The team was 3 programmers and 3 artists strong. I was the Project Leader and coordinated most of the meetings and development targets. I worked on every major system at least once. The hardest challenge was the networking of the game and making sure that the client and the server did not desync. I also wrote a little bit about failing our way towards an usable User Interface. Ultimate Parkour Challenge

 Run,Jump,Long Jump, High Jump, Slide, Dash, Swing, WallRun and WallDash your way to the goal. In this epic jump & run the focus is on the expressive moveset. You will have to chain together different moves and all that while trying to dodge different types of obstacles along the way. From concept to finish me and a second programmer spend about 3 months working on this prototype. I mostly worked on the character controller and spend a lot of my time to get the different moves feel right and transition to each other smoothly. This project allowed me to solidify my linear algebra skills because each state in someway needs to know a angle,direction or position.
